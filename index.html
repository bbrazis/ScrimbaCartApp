How to Shop Cat

Add what you want or need to buy into the input field above and click the add to list button. That will add the item to your "buy" list.

After you make your purchase, you can click on the item and it will move to the "bought but not forgot" list. Where you can click it again to return the item back to your buy list.

This way you you don't have to add the same items over and over again. If you don't want the item to show up anymore, you can click on the red "X" button for it to be removed completely.
